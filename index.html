Battleground

A crowd gaming app built to encourage spontaneous social interaction. After signing in with a Facebook or Google account, background geolocation data starts streaming and is compared against other players in real-time. When two players are within 5 meters they are prompted to take part in a duel. Using their phone like a sword, a winner is determined and points are applied to a global leaderboard.

React Native, iOS, Rails API, PostgreSQL, PostGIS, Geolocation, Accelerometer, Facebook Login, Google Sign-In

Meteor 404

Coordinates for just over 1,000 unrecovered meteorites are loaded from the NASA API and converted into an address displayed on Google Maps. Type in your current location and you will receive directions to the closest one. This app enables you to search for real space rocks that have never been recovered.

Full Stack Development, Javascript, HTML/CSS, Google Maps Geocoding API, Google Maps Directions API
