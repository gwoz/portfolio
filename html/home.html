Direct Swap

An electronic marketplace for companies who use derivatives to manage foreign exchange, interest rate, and commodity risk. Companies use Direct Swap to streamline their hedging process and discover new sources of liquidity.

Battleground

A crowd gaming iPhone app built in React Native using a Rails API and Postgres geospatial-enabled database. After signing in using Facebook or Google, geolocation data starts streaming and is compared against other users in real-time. When two users are within 5 meters they are prompted to take part in a duel where phone accelerometer data is used to determine a winner.

Meteor 404

A web application built in Rails using the NASA Meteorite Landings API, Google Maps Geocoding API, and Google Maps Directions API. Coordinate and other data for just over 1,000 confirmed but unrecovered meteorites is loaded from the NASA API, converted into an address with the Google Geocoding API, and then directions to that location are generated with the Google Maps Directions API. This app enables you to go find a new meteor and return a Meteor 200!
